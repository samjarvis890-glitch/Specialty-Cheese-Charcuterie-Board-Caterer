Delivery Information

How we ensure your masterpiece arrives perfectly intact.

Safe Transport

All boards are transported in climate-controlled catering vans using specialized level-racks to prevent any shifting of the delicate ingredients.

On-Site Setup

For Grazing Tables, we arrive 1.5 to 2 hours prior to your event start time to assemble the landscape fresh on-site for maximum visual impact.

Cleanup & Retrieval

If you rented our elevated props or wooden boards, our team will return within 24 hours of your event's conclusion to retrieve our equipment.
